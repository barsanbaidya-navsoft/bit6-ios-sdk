Introduction

Bit6 is a real-time, cloud-based platform for developers. It allows quickly and easily add voice/video calling, texting, and multimedia messaging capabilities to applications.

In addition Bit6 provides a suite of essential services including Identity Management, Social Map, and Virtual Wallet to help integrate the communication capabilities into a complete end-user experience.
